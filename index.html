About this Site

Welcome! I hope that this page offers you some useful tools that will help you find books that you love. There will be three main sections to this site, as shown below:

Choosing the Right Book

This section will help you to find the right book based on reading level and interest. Also, surprisingly, this page will let you know when to put a book down that just isn't working for you. There's nothing worse than forcing yourself to read a book that you're not enjoying!

Reading Recommendations

This section will help you identify that kinds of books you like and help you find interesting titles! It can be easy to get overwhelmed with all the books that are our there, but when you find out how to narrow them down, choosing the right books becomes pretty easy.

Tracking Your Reading

It's incredibly important to keep track of what you like and DON'T like about books. This way, you can pick out your future reads even easier! This section will give you some helpful tools to guide you in tracking what you've read. This site was created by Lacey Sexton, teacher at Menifee Central Elementary in Frenchburg, KY.
