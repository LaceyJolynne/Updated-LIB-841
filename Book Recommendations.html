Choosing Books You Will Like

Books comes in many shapes and sizes. For many people, the easiest way to finding a new book you will like is to figure out which Genres you like best. A genre is a group of books that a similar. For example, romance books have to deal with relationships while mystery books tend to deal with solving a riddle.
